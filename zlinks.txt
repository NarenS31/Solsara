Honest advice on how to use these
Don't watch full tutorials start to finish. Watch the first 5-10 minutes to understand the concept, then come back here and we'll write the actual code together tailored to your specific project. Full tutorials will show you generic examples that don't match your structure and you'll spend hours trying to adapt them.
The pattern for each phase should be:

Watch 10 mins of the relevant video to understand the concept
Come back here
We write it together for your specific codebase
You run it, tell me what breaks
We fix it
